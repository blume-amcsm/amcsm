# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We aim to create a welcoming, respectful, and inclusive environment for everyone interested in AMCSM.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intentions.
- Help others learn and grow.
- Keep discussions on-topic and relevant.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment or discrimination
- Personal attacks
- Spam or disruptive behavior
- Sharing private information without consent

## Reporting Issues

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please open a confidential issue or contact the repository maintainer directly.

## Enforcement

Violations may result in warnings or removal from the project community.

Thank you for helping maintain a positive environment.
